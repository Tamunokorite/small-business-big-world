About Us

Founded in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja, Nigeria in 2016, Small Business Big World can simply be described as an ideas, projects, and experiential events incubator. We are a boutique creative agency with strengths in project ideation, design, strategy marketing, project management, capacity building, event activations, art shows, AGMs, high networth individuals profiling, and business consultation.

We are a community of young professionals and creatives with a combined history of experience spanning over 15 years in the industry.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

Doosuur Tilley-Gyado is the visionary Founder & Lead Strategist of Small Business Big World - an ideas, projects, and execution incubator that houses a vibrant community of young professional creatives.

As a progressive community builder, social impact advocate, and thought leader within the creative space, Doosuur has a proven track record of engaging in compassionate and socially conscious entrepreneurship.

With a BSc in Sociology and BA in International Development, both from the Universities of Essex and Sussex UK, her strengths have been finely honed and distilled but are by no means limited to project management, HNI Management, team building, conflict resolution and brand consulting.

Doosuur is a seasoned international and cross-cultural professional, with over a decade of high level project management expertise spanning the creative industry, political space, documentary productions and corporate events to mention a few.

A fruitful career as a record label executive at Chocolate City, Audu Maikori (Group Chairman), saw Doosuur managing world-renowned artists such as M.I Abaga, Jesse Jagz, Iceprince, and Brymo, which allowed her to develop her keen interest in working closely with "hybrid" Africans on their self actualization journey.

Doosuur’s ideas about Africa, and Africans is at the core of SBBW’s existence; it is why she also provides leadership and entrepreneur coaching as well as people management training programs because she believes that of all her achievements, coaching/counselling hybrid Africans on a path of accelerated personal self development, will contribute to, and boost the emergence of an Afropolitan Africa, where young minds are proferring solutions to the world’s problems.
